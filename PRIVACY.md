# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-09

## Overview

Lingko is designed with privacy at its core. We don't collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data.

## Data Collection

We don't collect any personal data.

## Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and can be deleted by removing the app.

## Third-Party Services

The app uses Apple's on-device translation frameworks. No third-party services access your data.

## Changes to This Policy

We'll update this policy if our practices change. Continued use means acceptance of updates.
